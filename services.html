How We Teach

Two Ways to Learn

1-on-1 Tutoring

Personalized Instruction

Just you and your tutor. Every session is built around your specific challenges, your pace, and your goals.

Best for: Students who need focused attention, test prep, or are working through a difficult concept.

Group Learning

Peer Collaboration

Small groups at the same level working through the same subject together. Groups are kept tight so no one gets lost.

Best for: Students who do better when they can talk through problems with peers.

In Person or Online
