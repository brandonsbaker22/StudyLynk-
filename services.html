How We Teach

Two Ways to Learn

1-on-1 Tutoring

Personalized Instruction

Just you and your tutor. Every session is built around your specific challenges, your pace, and your goals. No distractions, no waiting for others to catch up.

Best for: Students who need focused attention, test prep, or are working through a difficult concept.

Group Learning

Peer Collaboration

Small groups of students at the same level working through the same subject together. You learn from your tutor and from each other. Groups are kept tight so no one gets lost.

Best for: Students who do better when they can talk through problems with peers and build a study community.

What Is a Workshop?

A workshop is a short, focused, hands-on session, usually 2 hours to a full day. You come in, learn one specific skill, practice it, and leave with something useful. No long commitment required. Great for people who want to try something new or pick up a skill quickly.

2 hours ‚Äì 1 full day

What Is a Course?

A course is a structured multi-session learning experience that runs over 2 to 3 weeks. Sessions build on each other so you go deeper into the subject over time. Think of it like a community college class but more accessible, more hands-on, and built around your schedule.
