Welcome to StudyLynk
Building Knowledge Together

We connect students with experienced tutors for personalized 1-on-1 and group tutoring sessions in Bentonville, AR and online. Our mission is to make sure every student has the support they need to succeed, because understanding shouldn’t depend on luck, privilege, or knowing the right person.
