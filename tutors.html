Founder & Lead Tutor

Where it all started.

Founder · StudyLynk

Brandon Scott

Founder & IT / Web Development Specialist

Technology and gaming were everything to Brandon growing up. While other kids were outside, he was inside taking things apart, figuring out how they worked, and putting them back together. Hardware, software, coding, HTML. It all fascinated him. Computers were not just tools, they were a whole world he wanted to understand completely.

That same curiosity made him the person everyone came to for help. Stuck on a coding project? Call Brandon. Computer acting up? Brandon knew what to do. He had a way of breaking down IT concepts and explaining things in plain language that made it click for people. Helping others understand technology came naturally to him and he loved doing it.

As a tutor Brandon specializes in IT, web development, coding, and technology. He works with students at every level from complete beginners who have never written a line of code to students working through advanced coursework. His approach is simple — no rushing, no judgment, just clear explanations until it makes sense.

The Tutors

Real people. Real stories.

Sarah Martinez

English, Writing & Literature
Writing Grammar Literature

Sarah has had a love for words since she could pick up a book. English, writing, and literature were never just subjects to her. They were a way of making sense of the world. She learned early on that everyone has something worth saying, but not everyone knows how to say it yet. That is where she comes in.

She works with students who feel like writing is their weakest subject and helps them realize it does not have to be. Whether it is essay structure, grammar, or just finding the confidence to put thoughts on paper, Sarah meets students where they are and helps them find their voice one sentence at a time.

Joseph Gonzalez

Math & Science
Algebra Calculus Biology Chemistry

Math and science have always come naturally to Joseph, but what he is really good at is explaining them to people they do not come naturally to. He studied engineering and spent years watching students give up on subjects they were actually capable of. Not because they were not smart, but because nobody explained it in a way that made sense to them.

Joseph does not rush and he does not judge. He breaks concepts down step by step, finds the approach that works for that specific student, and stays with them until it clicks. Algebra, geometry, calculus, biology, chemistry. He has helped students through all of it.

Marcus Stone

STEM & Computer Engineering
Physics Programming Engineering

Marcus has been passionate about STEM and computer engineering for as long as he can remember. There is something about solving a hard problem by working through it piece by piece until the answer finally comes together. That is something he has always loved. And he wants students to feel that same satisfaction instead of giving up before they get there.

He specializes in making STEM subjects feel less intimidating. Physics, programming, engineering concepts. Marcus has a way of making the complex feel manageable and helping students build the confidence to tackle problems they never thought they could solve.

Ante Samarzija

History & Social Studies
U.S. History World History Civics

Ante believes history is one of the most misunderstood subjects in school. Not because it is hard, but because it is too often taught as a list of dates and facts instead of what it really is. A story about people. When you understand why things happened and who was affected, history stops feeling like something you have to memorize and starts feeling like something you actually want to know.

That is the approach Ante brings to every session. Whether it is U.S. history, world history, civics, or social studies, he connects the material to real events and real consequences so students walk away understanding it and not just remembering it long enough for the test.

Christina Reyes

Business & Study Skills
Time Management Note-Taking Test Prep

Christina knows that being smart is not enough on its own. You can understand the material and still fall behind if you do not have the right systems in place. How you manage your time, how you organize your notes, how you prepare for a test, how you handle everything being due at once. These are skills nobody teaches you, and she thinks that is a problem.

With a background in business, Christina specializes in helping students build the habits and strategies that make school feel less overwhelming. She gives students tools they can use immediately and keep using long after the tutoring sessions are over.

Lance Reis

Reading & Learning Strategies
Comprehension Critical Thinking Learning Strategies

Lance has spent years working with students who struggle with reading and learning, and the one thing he knows for certain is that there is no single way to teach it. Every student processes information differently, and the approach that works for one person may do nothing for another. Finding the right method for each individual student is the whole job and it is one he takes seriously.

He focuses on reading comprehension, critical thinking, and building learning strategies that actually stick. Lance does not just help students get through the next assignment. He helps them become better, more confident learners in every subject.

Albert Dera

Science & Critical Thinking
Biology Chemistry AP Science

Albert has a deep passion for science and an even deeper passion for making sure students actually understand it. He has seen too many students walk out of a science class having memorized enough to pass a test but having no real grasp of what any of it means and that bothers him. Science is not about memorization. It is about understanding how things work and being able to think through problems you have never seen before.

Albert focuses on building that kind of thinking. Biology, chemistry, AP science prep. He covers it all, but his real goal in every session is to help students leave thinking more clearly than when they walked in.
