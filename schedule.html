Our Services & Availability

StudyLynk operates by appointment. All tutoring sessions are scheduled after a free consultation so we can match you with the right tutor and build a plan around your actual needs.

  1. Business Hours & Contact Availability

    These are the hours when our team is available to answer calls, respond to emails, and schedule free consultations.

    • Monday – Friday:  7:00am – 7:00pm
    • Saturday:  7:00am – 2:00pm
    • Sunday:  Closed
    • Phone:  (479) 616-9465
    • Email:  studylynk@gmail.com  (responses within 24 hours)

  2. Free Consultation — Always the First Step

    Before any tutoring sessions are scheduled, every student starts with a free 30-minute consultation. This is how we learn what you need and match you with the right tutor.

    • Duration:  30 minutes
    • Cost:  Free — no commitment required
    • Available:  Monday – Friday, 7:00am – 7:00pm  |  Saturday, 7:00am – 2:00pm
    • Format:  In person in Bentonville, AR or online via Zoom
    • How to book:  Through the Contact page or by calling (479) 616-9465

  3. One-on-One Tutoring

    Every student is matched with a tutor based on their subject needs and learning style after the free consultation. Sessions are 60 minutes and scheduled directly between you and your tutor — in person in Bentonville, AR or online via Zoom.

    • Brandon Scott — Technology & Coding, AI Tools, Web Design
    • Sarah Martinez — English, Writing & Literature, Essay Writing, Grammar, College Prep
    • Joseph Gonzalez — Math & Science, Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Biology, Chemistry
    • Marcus Stone — STEM & Computer Engineering, Physics, Problem Solving
    • Ante Samarzija — History & Social Studies, World History, U.S. History, Civics
    • Christina Reyes — Business & Study Skills, Time Management, Test Prep
    • Lance Reis — Reading & Learning Strategies, Reading Comprehension, Critical Thinking
    • Albert Dera — Science & Critical Thinking, Biology, Chemistry, AP Science Prep
    • Session times are set with your tutor after your free consultation.

  4. Group Study Sessions

    Small, focused groups of students working through the same subject together. Groups are formed based on shared subject needs identified during consultations — kept small so every student gets real attention. When two or more tutors cover the same subject, multiple groups may run at the same time. If a group reaches capacity, students can be added to a new group or moved to one-on-one sessions.

    • Math & Science — Led by Joseph Gonzalez or Albert Dera.
    • English & Writing — Led by Sarah Martinez.
    • History & Social Studies — Led by Ante Samarzija.
    • STEM & Technology — Led by Brandon Scott or Marcus Stone.
    • Study Skills & Test Prep — Led by Christina Reyes or Lance Reis. SAT/ACT prep groups and general study skills groups are kept separate.
    • If your preferred tutor’s group is full, you’ll be placed in the next available group with that tutor. In the meantime, we can match you with another tutor who is currently hosting a session or offer a one-on-one session to keep you moving forward. Group schedules are finalized once the group is formed. To join an existing group or be added to the waitlist, please contact us.
