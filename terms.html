🎯 Student Conduct & Expectations

At StudyLynk, we believe that great learning happens in an environment of mutual respect and focus. We ask that all students:

  • Come prepared: Bring necessary materials, completed homework, and questions to discuss
  • Be on time: Sessions start at the scheduled time. Arriving late cuts into your learning time
  • Stay focused: Put away distractions like phones and games during sessions unless specifically needed for learning
  • Engage respectfully: Ask questions, share your thoughts, and treat your tutor with the same respect you would expect in return
  • Communicate openly: Let us know if something is not working or if you need to adjust your approach
  • Take ownership: Tutoring works best when you are actively involved in your own learning

We're here to support you, not judge you. If you're struggling with focus, motivation, or anything else, that's exactly what we're here for. Just talk to us about it.

📅 Session Changes & Cancellations

Life happens. We get it. Here's how we handle changes to your tutoring schedule:

Rescheduling Your Session
  • 24 hours notice: If you need to reschedule, let us know at least 24 hours in advance. We'll work with you to find a new time that fits both your schedule and your tutor's
  • Less than 24 hours: We understand emergencies happen. Contact us immediately and we'll do our best to accommodate you
  • How to request: Call us at (479) 616-9465 or email studylynk@gmail.com with your new preferred times
Canceling a Session
  • One-time cancellation: No problem. Just let us know as soon as you know you can't make it
  • Ongoing changes: If you need to pause or stop sessions, we're happy to discuss what's working and what isn't. Your feedback helps us serve you better

Our goal is to work with you, not against you. If your situation changes, talk to us. We'd rather adjust your plan than have you stuck in something that doesn't work anymore.

🚌 Field Trips & Parent Involvement

Our field trips are designed to bring learning to life. For younger students especially, we encourage parent and guardian involvement.

We Want Your Help

If you're interested in joining a field trip as a parent or guardian helper, please let us know! Whether it's a classroom visit, a local exploration, or a hands-on learning experience, extra adult supervision and support makes these trips better for everyone.

  • Contact us at (479) 616-9465 or studylynk@gmail.com
  • Let us know which trips interest you and what you can help with
  • We'll provide details about timing, location, and what to expect
  • Your involvement shows your student that learning matters to you too
Field Trip Guidelines
  • All participants must follow school/venue safety rules
  • Students should come prepared with water, appropriate clothing, and any materials we've mentioned
  • Parent helpers should arrive on time and stay for the duration of the trip
  • We focus on learning, but we also want everyone to have fun and feel safe

⚖️ General Terms & Policies

Payment & Billing

Payment is due at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made. We accept cash, card, and digital payments. If you have questions about pricing or need to discuss payment options, contact us.

Confidentiality

Your student's progress, grades, and personal information are confidential. We don't share details about your sessions or your student's performance with anyone except you (the parent/guardian) and your student. The only exception is if we're required to do so by law.

Liability

StudyLynk provides tutoring services in good faith. While we work hard to help your student succeed, we cannot guarantee specific grades or test scores. Learning is a partnership between the student, the tutor, and the family. Success depends on effort, engagement, and time.

Changes to These Policies

We may update these policies from time to time. When we do, we'll let you know. Your continued use of our services means you agree to the updated policies.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about these policies or concerns about your tutoring experience, please reach out. We're here to listen and to make things right.
